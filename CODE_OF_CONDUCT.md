# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, technically grounded collaboration.

Participants should:

- communicate clearly and in good faith
- focus on observable behavior and technical facts
- keep disagreement constructive
- avoid harassment, hostility, and personal attacks

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or moderate content that makes the project harder to collaborate in safely and productively.

## Scope

This applies to issues, discussions, pull requests, and other project communication channels.
